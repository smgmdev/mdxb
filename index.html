Get to know more

We offer fixed and variable rate mortgages for villas, apartments, townhouses, and off-plan properties across Dubai. Enjoy competitive rates from 3.99%, financing up to 80% for UAE residents (75% for non-residents), and flexible repayment terms of up to 25 years.
You will need: valid passport and UAE visa, Emirates ID, salary certificate or proof of income, 6 months of bank statements, property details or MOU (Memorandum of Understanding), and proof of down payment funds.
Rates start from 3.99% p.a. for fixed-rate plans and EIBOR + 1.5% for variable. Processing fee is 1% of the loan amount (min. AED 5,000). Property valuation fee is AED 2,500–3,500. No early settlement fees after 3 years on select plans.

*You may be eligible for online pre-approval if you meet the following criteria:

  • UAE resident or non-resident with valid passport;
  • Minimum monthly income of AED 15,000 (salaried) or AED 25,000 (self-employed);
  • Property located within Dubai or Abu Dhabi;
  • Maximum loan-to-value of 80% for residents, 75% for non-residents; AND
  • Property value of AED 500,000 or above

Non-residents may be required to provide additional documentation or attend a video consultation with one of our mortgage advisors.

*Subject to eligibility, bank approval, and upon providing all required documents. Rates and terms may vary by lender. Terms and conditions apply.
